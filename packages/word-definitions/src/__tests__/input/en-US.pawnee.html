Pawnee

noun

Paw·​nee pȯ-ˈnē 
pä-
plural Pawnee or Pawnees
: a member of an American Indian people originally of Kansas and Nebraska

Word History

Etymology

of Siouan origin; akin to Osage ppáį Pawnee, Omaha ppáðį

First Known Use

1698, in the meaning defined above

Time Traveler
